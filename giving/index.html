Stewardship

If you have questions about giving, about our spending or anything related to money, please do not hesitate to reach out to any of our Elders on a Sunday morning. We welcome these discussions and we would love to talk with you.

As a non-profit entity, all monies donated to our church are utilized for the advancement of our ministry. Donations to Redeemer are utilized in one of three areas: Operating Expenses, Staff Salaries and Church Planting/Missions. All financial decisions are made by our Elders, with advice and counsel from a 7 member Finance Committee. We operate on an annual budget that is periodically adjusted by the Elders and Finance Committee to reflect our changing operational needs. The Elders and Finance committee are provided with weekly financial status updates and all members and attendees are provided with a monthly church update. In this manner, the leaders, members and attendees of the church have appropriate insight into our expenses and revenues on a regular basis.
